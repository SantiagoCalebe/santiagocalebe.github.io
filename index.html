About Me.

My name is Santiago, a young Brazilian programmer who began his professional journey in 2020. I took my first steps in the field of technology through studying and practicing HTML, dedicating myself to understanding the fundamentals of web development. Although this initial experience provided me with a valuable foundation, I soon realized that my ambitions extended beyond the creation of simple web pages.

From an early stage, I developed a strong interest in exploring broader areas of programming and computer science. While HTML introduced me to the structure of the web and logical thinking in development, I aspired to deepen my knowledge in more complex technologies, programming languages, and software development concepts. My goal has always been to move beyond basic front-end structures and engage with more advanced topics, such as back-end development, application design, and problem-solving through code.

Since then, I have continued to pursue learning opportunities that challenge my skills and expand my technical perspective. As a teenager passionate about technology, I remain committed to continuous growth, innovation, and the pursuit of excellence in programming.
